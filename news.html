Dr. Saridena Calls for Comprehensive Agricultural Reforms in Telangana

In a detailed press statement, Official Spokesperson Dr. Rahul Saridena outlined BJP Telangana's comprehensive agenda for agricultural transformation — including enhanced MSP implementation, Rythu Bandhu improvements, irrigation expansion, and market linkage support for over 60 lakh farmers in the state.

"Development is meaningful only when it reaches every farmer," he emphasized, calling for immediate policy corrections by the state government.
