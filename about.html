About Dr. Rahul Saridena

Bridging Policy, People and Progress — a leader grounded in knowledge, service, and accountability.

BJP Telangana
His Story

A Leader Born from Purpose

Dr. Rahul Saridena represents a leadership model grounded in knowledge, service, and accountability. Unlike conventional political communicators, his approach combines analytical thinking with grassroots understanding.

Whether addressing public meetings, participating in policy discussions, engaging with media platforms, or interacting with citizens across Telangana, his focus remains consistent — connecting governance with people's aspirations.

As the Official Spokesperson of BJP Telangana, he plays a key role in presenting the party's vision, communicating policy initiatives, and contributing to constructive public discourse on issues impacting Telangana and India.

As a writer and political commentator, he has authored analytical essays on contemporary politics, governance, and electoral strategy, bringing intellectual depth to political communication.

His public engagement reflects a balanced blend of data-driven analysis, developmental politics, and cultural pride.

"Development is meaningful only when it reaches every household, every farmer, every youth, and every citizen."

— Dr. Rahul Saridena
Leadership Roles

Positions Held

Dr. Saridena has served in key organizational roles within the Bharatiya Janata Party in Telangana.

Official Spokesperson — BJP Telangana

As the Official Spokesperson, Dr. Saridena is responsible for presenting the party's official positions, engaging with national and regional media, responding to political developments, and communicating BJP's governance narrative to citizens of Telangana.

Co-Convenor, State Protocol Cell — BJP Telangana

As Co-Convenor of the State Protocol Cell, he coordinates official party protocols, manages high-level delegations and state-level events, and ensures the smooth execution of BJP's organizational activities across Telangana.
